## Create a AWS Managed Microsoft AD  
<br><br>   

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249580-1d6d4fd4-0c8b-44a3-9ed2-7a3e10387ebe.png) 
#### 1. Click on `Directory Service` under Security, Identity & Compliance  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249623-7dfd434e-f9d2-401a-97eb-3c6fbd0530ce.png) 
#### 2. Click on `Set up directory`  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249655-4a1b544c-7919-4e09-9616-9f9979969cb2.png) 
#### 3. Choose AWS Managed Microsoft AD  
#### 4. Click on `Next`  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249705-1526439d-f070-4b94-a424-40aff8025dd0.png) 
#### 5. Choose Standard Edition  
#### 6. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249736-ec66c504-614e-447d-a04d-b94597ae198f.png) 
#### 7. Enter Directory DNS name  
#### 8. Enter Directory NetBIOS name 
#### 9. Enter description 
#### 10. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249752-cc9ef9c3-aff8-489e-a9b8-732ce68f61cb.png) 
#### 11. Enter admin password  
#### 12. Confirm password
#### 13. Click on `Next`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249778-0ff0b631-bf54-4836-9ce5-ec542f2fcb52.png) 
#### 14. Choose VPC  
#### 15. Choose subnet(s)
#### 16. Click on `Next` 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136249808-0a1a9de9-f3c6-4e02-9dcb-047d0ef2e50e.png) 
#### 17. Click on `Create directory`  
---
