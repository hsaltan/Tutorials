## Provision public certificate  
<br><br>   

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667251-1ba8b628-bd4d-479b-a08d-585fd9ef8018.png)
#### 1. Create a hosted zone as described in the above lab  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667258-efd7950f-ef02-4bd8-9846-b6e551ef314d.png)
#### 2. Go to the AWS console  
#### 3. Click on `Certificate Manager` under Security, Identity & Compliance   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667262-9e7d0ca5-86ed-4fc1-bb29-46f66420d8db.png)
#### 4. Click on `Get started`  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667264-c450fb9f-b68b-45cb-84c8-a2e7dc1a3250.png)
#### 5. Choose `Request a public certificate`  
#### 6. Click on `Request a certificate`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667270-c73b4cd2-b491-41a4-8ac0-bad3a84896d7.png)
#### 7. Enter domain name (_www.simpledataanalytics.com_)  
#### 8. Click on `Next`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667273-b8e29fb3-abe1-49dc-aac0-9cc71cd66636.png)
#### 9. Choose `Email validation`  
#### 10. Click on `Next`  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667282-52b45e6d-8322-42a8-85d7-735abb3a731f.png)
#### 11. Click on `Add Tag`  
#### 12. Enter tag(s)  
#### 13. Click on `Review`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667286-b3a33e0c-11db-4d1f-bdd5-36eb126eaddd.png)
#### 14. Click on `Confirm and request`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667294-877ad377-486f-4ca2-aa49-733ee278a3ae.png)
#### 15. Click on `Continue`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667299-574e2df0-ca53-4751-b9e9-68131d96b265.png)
#### 16. Go to your email inbox  
#### 17. Copy the URL link  
#### 18. Go to the web browser  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667308-c881271a-7577-4358-b928-1ca5fd587bbd.png)
#### 19. Paste it in the URL of the web browser  
#### 20. Click on `I Approve`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136667322-0ac78cc1-56d1-4fdc-bd37-79af2019cde0.png)
#### 21. See the success message  
---
