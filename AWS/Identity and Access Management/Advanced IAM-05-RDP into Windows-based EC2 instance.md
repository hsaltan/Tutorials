## RDP into Windows-based EC2 instance  
<br><br>   

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136257910-3282e2dc-4492-4c99-bcdf-d9dac89b4131.png)  
#### 1. Launch a Windows-based EC2 instance as described in the above lab
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136257936-5e5a154e-fec0-4b03-ade8-f73129e091a6.png)
#### 2. Choose the instance
#### 3. Click on `Actions`
#### 4. Click on `Connect`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136257969-0c129c6e-2436-420c-909c-b44e2b077128.png)
#### 5. Click on `RDP client`
#### 6. Click on `Get password`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136257996-792d9050-17e3-47fb-9aa5-7ee371dc5caf.png)
#### 7. Click on `Browse` and download the private key
#### 8. Click on `Decrypt Password`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258008-e5d1e5df-e675-4fa5-b34b-54cdaf3fd1ac.png)
#### 9. Copy public DNS, user name and password on notepad
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258038-d571be94-c7d6-4612-95da-8e41e3ca8011.png)
#### 10. Open Microsoft Remote Desktop
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258058-3463b90e-725c-4b87-9758-8c2facc5763a.png)
#### 11. Click on `Add PC`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258084-79d9f640-6ba1-466b-90a9-99096390e1c6.png)
#### 12. Go back to the instances page on AWS
#### 13. Copy the public IPv4 address of the EC2 instance
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258104-41291416-bcf3-4d1f-a2e3-a5f3dc515bdb.png)
#### 14. Paste the IPv4 address of the EC2 instance in PC name
#### 15. Scroll down
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258132-6d09a877-bcf1-4e57-b740-834775656a79.png)
#### 16. Click on `Add`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258147-6e1b60b4-4deb-42b2-a2d1-bde7c76dbba0.png)
#### 17. Double click on PC
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258167-43d79efd-cdef-4bd4-bbb0-82cfa24dd979.png)
#### 18. Enter username (it was on the notepad)
#### 19. Enter password (it was on the notepad)
#### 20. Click on `Continue`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258184-456b6f06-244e-4486-a958-85f65873e455.png)
#### 21. Click on `Continue`
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136258208-f8de5cc2-3b26-4e5f-b8a2-d2b973025886.png)
#### 22. See and check the windows PC
---
