## Use a SNS case  
<br><br>   

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136999936-213da7be-1fc3-440e-9b78-db833f124ff5.png)  
#### 1. On the AWS console, click on `Simple Notification Service` under Application Integration   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/136999974-06d7de50-5556-4152-bb70-4df5b567d4fc.png)  
#### 2. Enter a topic name (_MyTopic_)  
#### 3. Click on `Next step` 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000011-6c1ec9f7-5d31-416f-acef-ee65d856a19b.png)  
#### 4. Enter display name (_My Topic_)  
#### 5. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000050-3867f9f5-1e9c-459b-a60e-444a9816d9b4.png)  
#### 6. Enable encryption  
#### 7. Choose CMK (_default_) 
#### 8. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000084-4d035e1f-8f63-4112-94d6-966e7a465238.png)  
#### 9. Choose method (_Basic_)  
#### 10. Define the sender (_Only the topic owner_) 
#### 11. Define the receiver (_Only the topic owner_) 
#### 12. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000111-6b68b7e9-c5d8-4211-89ce-34da7749c68f.png)  
#### 13. Use the default delivery retry policy  
#### 14. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000131-fbf888e9-d517-4f4f-b5f6-7070514aa678.png)  
#### 15. Leave as default  
#### 16. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000170-a4b9bfb2-4b43-46a7-9f51-fc7a921a32e9.png)  
#### 17. Click on `Add tag`  
#### 18. Enter tag(s) 
#### 19. Click on `Create topic`  
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000206-1da9aad1-59a5-4222-b8c2-2dbe523952cb.png)  
#### 20. On the Subscriptions tab, click on `Create subscription`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000227-ae7046a4-e877-424e-9197-4606d6e8b13f.png)  
#### 21. Choose the protocol (_Email_)  
#### 22. Choose the endpoint (_email address_) 
#### 23. Scroll down 
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000247-d33d7b58-3944-46bf-b927-238f19713c7f.png)  
#### 24. Click on `Create subscription`   
---
![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/40828825/137000278-a77fc9d1-7811-4788-8abc-8fa6df44983c.png)  
#### 25. Go to your email inbox  
#### 26. Click on `Confirm subscription` 
---
